3. **Solves Real Pain Points**: The HN discussion on Antigravity (1088 points, 1085 comments) revealed users hitting rate limits within 20 minutes. This plugin's multi-account rotation directly addresses that frustration.

4. **OpenCode Ecosystem Integration**: OpenCode already had community mindshare as a terminal-based alternative to Antigravity. Appearing in [OpenCode's official ecosystem docs](https://opencode.ai/docs/ecosystem/) gave instant credibility.

## Context: Google Antigravity Launch

Google Antigravity was announced on **November 18, 2025** alongside Gemini 3. Key facts:

- AI-powered IDE (VSCode fork) with agentic development features
- Free preview with generous rate limits on Gemini 3 Pro
- Also provides access to Claude Sonnet 4.5 and Claude Opus 4.5
- HN reception was mixed - 1088 points but heavy criticism about being "just another VSCode fork"
- Users reported hitting rate limits within 20 minutes

This created the perfect storm for a plugin that:
1. Lets you use Antigravity's quotas from a terminal tool (OpenCode)
2. Rotates between multiple accounts to bypass rate limits
3. Accesses premium models (Claude Opus 4.5) for free
